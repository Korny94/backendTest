Haley Social Media App
Haley is a social media application that allows users to create posts, share images, comment on posts, react to posts, follow other users, view their content, send messages, and play games against each other.

Features
Create Posts: Users can create and publish their posts, including text and images.

Image Sharing: Share your favorite images and moments with your friends and followers.

Comments: Engage with others through comments on posts, fostering discussions and interactions.

Reactions: Express your feelings towards posts with various reaction options, including likes, dislikes, loves, and more.

Follow Other Users: Stay updated with your favorite users by following them and getting notified about their new posts.

View Profiles: Explore the profiles of other users to see their posts and information.

Messaging: Send and receive private messages to and from other users to keep your conversations private.

Games: Challenge your friends to games and have fun competing against each other.

Technologies Used
Frontend: The application is built from scratch using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, providing a modern and responsive user interface.

Backend: API calls are made to a headless WordPress site to fetch and store data. This decoupled architecture ensures data is efficiently managed and displayed in the application.
